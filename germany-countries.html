Tourism

Research and Innovation

For individuals engaged in research or innovation, Germany offers opportunities to collaborate with leading institutions and be part of a dynamic research community.

Business

For business travelers, Germany offers opportunities to engage in international trade, attend conferences, and establish business connections in one of Europe's leading economies.

Family Reunion

Family reunion visas permit individuals to join their family members already residing in Germany, fostering family unity.

Work Visas

Germany offers work visas for individuals seeking employment in the country. The type of work visa you may apply for depends on factors such as your qualifications, skill level, and the demand for your profession in Germany.

Student Visas

If you plan to study in Germany, you will need to apply for a student visa. This visa allows you to stay in Germany for the duration of your studies.

Family Reunification

Individuals who are residents in Germany can apply for family reunification to bring their family members to join them. This applies to spouses, children, and in some cases, parents.

Permanent Residency

After living in Germany for a certain period under a temporary residence permit (such as a work or student visa), you may be eligible to apply for a settlement permit, which allows you to live and work in Germany indefinitely.a

Health Insurance

Health insurance is mandatory for all residents in Germany, including immigrants. You will need to provide proof of health insurance when applying for a visa.

Language Requirements

Depending on the type of visa, you may need to demonstrate proficiency in the German language, especially for work and settlement permits.

Job Seeker Visa

The Job Seeker Visa allows individuals to come to Germany and search for a job. Once you find employment, you can then switch to a work visa.

Short-Stay Business Visa

If you plan to stay in Germany for up to 90 days within a 180-day period for business purposes, you will generally need to apply for a short-stay business visa, also known as a Schengen Visa

Eligibility

Business visas are typically granted for activities such as attending conferences, business meetings, negotiations, trade fairs, and other professional events.

Application Process

The application process involves submitting the required documents, including a letter of invitation from the German business partner, proof of accommodation, flight itinerary, travel insurance, and other supporting documents.

Travel Insurance

Travel insurance that covers medical expenses and repatriation for the entire duration of your intended stay is usually a requirement

Proof of Financial Means

You may be required to provide evidence of sufficient funds to cover your stay in Germany.

Letter of Invitation

A letter of invitation from the German business partner or host organization, outlining the purpose and duration of your visit, is often necessary.

Supporting Documents

Additional documents may include proof of employment, a no-objection certificate from your employer, and any other documents relevant to your business activities.

Biometric Data

Biometric data (fingerprints) is often collected as part of the visa application process.

Trade and Investment Promotions

The Australian government actively promotes trade and investment. Various agencies, such as Austrade (Australian Trade and Investment Commission), provide support and information for businesses looking to expand into or invest in Australia.

Eligibility

You must intend to visit Australia for tourism, family visits, or other short-term non-work purposes.

Duration

The duration of stay allowed varies, but it is generally granted for 3, 6, or 12 months.

Multiple or Single Entry

The visa can be granted for a single entry or multiple entries during the validity period.

Work Limitations

This visa does not allow you to work in Australia, but it permits activities such as tourism, visiting family and friends, or other short-term non-work activities.

Application Process

The application process typically involves submitting an online application, providing supporting documents, and paying the applicable visa fee.

Health and Character Requirements

You might be required to undergo health examinations, and you must meet character requirements.

No Further Stay Condition

Some visas may have a "No Further Stay" condition, which means you cannot apply for another visa while you are in Australia. Make sure to check the conditions on your visa.

Electronic Travel Authority

Some passport holders might be eligible for an Electronic Travel Authority (ETA) or eVisitor, which are electronically linked to your passport and do not require a visa label.
