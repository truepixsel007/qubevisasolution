Innovator Visa

The Innovator Visa is designed for experienced entrepreneurs who wish to establish a business in the UK. Applicants need to have an innovative, viable, and scalable business idea, and they must be endorsed by an approved endorsing body.

Start-up Visa

The Start-up Visa is for individuals looking to start a business in the UK for the first time. It is aimed at those with innovative business ideas but who do not meet the investment requirements of the Innovator Visa.

Eligibility Criteria

Both the Innovator and Start-up Visas have specific eligibility criteria, and applicants must meet these criteria to be considered for a visa.

Business Plan

A comprehensive and realistic business plan is a crucial component of the visa application process. The plan should outline the business idea, its potential for innovation or growth, and how it will meet the endorsement criteria.

Endorsing Bodies

The choice of endorsing body is essential, and applicants should carefully select one that aligns with their business sector and goals.

Application Process

The application process involves submitting the required documentation, including the business plan, proof of endorsement, and other supporting documents.

Changes to Immigration System

The UK has introduced a new points-based immigration system. Depending on when you are reading this, there may have been changes to the visa categories and requirements.

Investment Funds

The amount of investment funds required can vary based on your specific situation. It may depend on whether you are setting up a new business or taking over an existing one.

Standard Visitor Visa

The Standard Visitor Visa is the most common visa for individuals intending to visit the UK for tourism purposes.

Eligibility Criteria

You must demonstrate that you intend to visit the UK for tourism purposes, such as sightseeing, visiting friends or family, or attending events.

Application Process

You will need to provide details about your travel plans, accommodation arrangements, and financial situation.

Supporting Documents

Commonly required documents include a valid passport, travel itinerary, proof of accommodation, proof of funds, and any documents supporting the purpose of your visit.

Visa Processing Time

Visa processing times can vary, so it's advisable to apply well in advance of your planned travel date.

Visa Fee

There is typically a fee associated with the Standard Visitor Visa application. The fee can vary depending on the length of your intended stay.

Visa Exemptions

Some individuals may be exempt from needing a visa, depending on their nationality. It's essential to check whether you qualify for a visa exemption.

Departure

Ensure that you depart the U.K. before the expiration of your authorized stay to avoid any immigration issues.
